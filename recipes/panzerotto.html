Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water and eggs
  2. Stretch the mix so that it forms a circle
  3. Put tomato sauce and cheese at the center of the circle
  4. Take two edges and close the circle by folding it in half
  5. Put in the oven at 220Â°C for 2 hours
