Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water and eggs
  2. Make sheets from the mix you created beforehand
  3. Put them one above the other alternating with tomato sauce and meat
  4. Put in the oven at 220°C for 2 hours
